SOW - Statement of Work
The project-level contract defining scope, responsibilities, deliverables, and service commitments

Scope of Services

The SOW specifies each service item delivered: app integration, technical support, maintenance cycles, and phase-based deliverables. All scope changes require a signed Change Order addendum before implementation begins.

Data Security Commitment

All merchant data is processed under strict data minimization principles. Omegatheme does not use customer data for any purpose outside the agreed SOW scope. All personnel accessing data must sign individual NDAs and complete annual security training.

Service Level Agreement (SLA) Commitment

Minimum uptime of 99.9% per calendar month. P1 incident response within 1 hour; P2 within 4 hours. All planned maintenance is communicated at least 72 hours in advance via email and in-app notification.

Information Security & System Protection

Systems undergo scheduled vulnerability scanning and annual penetration testing by an independent third party. Access logs are retained for a minimum of 12 months. The incident response plan is activated within 2 hours of any confirmed security breach, including customer notification within 72 hours as required by GDPR.

Support Contact Mechanism

Support is available via 24/7 helpdesk ticket portal, business-hours email, and dedicated Slack channel for Enterprise customers. Each customer is assigned a named Delivery Manager as the primary point of contact for all escalations and coordination.

Complaint Handling Obligations

All complaints are acknowledged within 4 business hours. A formal written response is provided within 2 business days. If resolution is not reached, the customer may escalate to the designated Business Head per the escalation matrix documented in the SOW. All complaints are logged and tracked to closure.

Business Heads & Delivery Manager Contacts

The SOW formally names the Delivery Manager, Business Head, and Customer Success contact on both sides. These contacts are responsible for performance reviews, SLA reporting, and handling all operational and legal queries during the engagement.

DPA - Data Processing Agreement
Data processing terms aligned with GDPR and international data protection law

Data Controller / Processor Roles

The Customer acts as Data Controller - determining purposes and means of processing. Omegatheme acts as Data Processor - processing data solely on documented instructions from the Controller, with no independent use rights.

Sub-processor Management

Omegatheme maintains a publicly available sub-processor list. 30 days' advance notice is provided before any addition or replacement of sub-processors. Customers retain the right to object to new sub-processors within the notice period.

Data Subject Rights

Omegatheme assists Controllers in responding to Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs), including rights to access, rectification, erasure, and portability. Responses are facilitated within 72 hours of request receipt.

Cross-border Data Transfers

Transfers outside the EEA are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as updated by the European Commission. Omegatheme maintains Transfer Impact Assessments (TIAs) for all major data flows.

Breach Notification

In the event of a confirmed personal data breach, Omegatheme notifies the Controller within 72 hours of becoming aware. Notification includes the nature of the breach, categories of data affected, and mitigation steps taken.

Audit Rights

Customers have the right to audit Omegatheme's data processing activities once per calendar year upon 30 days' written notice, or at any time following a confirmed security incident. Costs are shared equally for routine audits.

Download the DPA: The full Data Processing Agreement including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) is available upon request. Email contact@omegatheme.com with your company name and the applicable service scope.

Availability Commitments & Definitions
How we define, measure, and report uptime

Uptime Calculation

Uptime is calculated as: (Total minutes in month − Unplanned downtime minutes) / Total minutes × 100. Planned maintenance windows, scheduled and communicated 72+ hours in advance, are excluded from downtime calculations.

SLA Credit Policy

If monthly uptime falls below 99.9%, affected customers are entitled to service credits. Credits are calculated as a percentage of monthly fees: 99.0%-99.9% = 10% credit; 95.0%-99.0% = 25% credit; below 95.0% = 50% credit. Credits must be requested within 30 days.

Disaster Recovery

Recovery Point Objective (RPO): ≤ 24 hours. Recovery Time Objective (RTO): ≤ 4 hours. Daily automated backups with 30-day retention are stored in geographically separate regions. DR drills are conducted semi-annually.

Maintenance Windows

Planned maintenance is scheduled during low-traffic hours (typically 02:00-06:00 UTC on weekends). All maintenance is announced via email and status page at least 72 hours in advance. Emergency patches may be applied with 2 hours' notice in cases of critical security vulnerabilities.

Monitoring & Alerting

All services are monitored 24/7 via automated health checks at 1-minute intervals. The on-call engineering team is paged immediately upon detection of any P1/P2 condition. Customers receive proactive status updates every 30 minutes during active incidents.

How We Protect Your Data
Technical and organizational measures applied across all Omegatheme products and infrastructure

Encryption at Rest

All merchant data stored in our systems is encrypted using AES-256. Database-level encryption is applied across all environments including staging and production.

Encryption in Transit

All data in transit is protected with TLS 1.3. We enforce HSTS on all public endpoints and reject any connections below TLS 1.2.

Access Control (RBAC)

Role-based access control ensures employees only access data required for their role. All privileged access requires MFA and is logged and reviewed quarterly.

Continuous Monitoring

24/7 automated monitoring with anomaly detection. All access logs, API calls, and admin actions are captured in immutable audit logs retained for 12 months.

Vulnerability Management

Automated vulnerability scanning runs weekly. Annual penetration testing is conducted by accredited third-party security firms. Critical patches are applied within 24 hours of discovery.

Backup & Recovery

Automated daily backups with 30-day retention, stored in geographically separate regions. RPO ≤ 24h, RTO ≤ 4h. DR plans are tested semi-annually.

Data Residency & Sub-processors
Where your data is stored and which third parties may process it on our behalf

Primary Data Residency

Production data is hosted on AWS infrastructure in the US-East and AP-Southeast regions. Enterprise customers may request dedicated region configuration (EU, US, or APAC) subject to plan eligibility.

Sub-processor Disclosure

Omegatheme uses a limited set of vetted sub-processors including cloud infrastructure (AWS), payment processing (Stripe), transactional email (SendGrid), and error monitoring (Sentry). The full, current sub-processor list is available on request and updated with 30 days' notice.

Cross-border Transfer Safeguards

All data transfers outside the EEA are governed by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). Transfer Impact Assessments (TIAs) are maintained for all major third-party data flows and reviewed annually.

Data Retention Policy

Active customer data is retained for the duration of the contract plus 90 days post-termination. After this period, data is securely deleted using NIST SP 800-88 compliant methods. Deletion certificates are provided upon request.

Security Program Highlights
Key controls and practices that underpin our compliance posture

Annual Penetration Testing

Independent third-party penetration tests are conducted annually covering web applications, APIs, and internal infrastructure. Findings are remediated within 30 days (critical) or 90 days (high/medium).

Vendor Security Assessment

All third-party vendors and sub-processors undergo security review before onboarding. Vendors with access to customer data must demonstrate equivalent security controls and sign DPAs.

Employee Security Training

Annual mandatory security awareness training for all employees. Role-specific training for engineers (secure coding, OWASP Top 10) and customer-facing staff (phishing, social engineering).

Incident Response Plan

Documented IRP covering detection, containment, eradication, and recovery phases. The plan is tested via tabletop exercises semi-annually. Customer notification is triggered within 72 hours of a confirmed breach.

Change Management

All production changes go through a formal review process: code review, automated testing, staging validation, and approvals before deployment. Emergency changes require post-deployment review.

Business Continuity Planning

BCP covering scenarios including data center outage, key-person dependency, and supply chain disruption. Plans are reviewed and updated annually and tested via annual DR drills.

Complaint & Escalation Process
How we handle feedback, disputes, and formal complaints under the SOW
STEP 01

Submit via Helpdesk or Email

Log your complaint or concern through the helpdesk portal or email contact@omegatheme.com. Include a clear description, any relevant screenshots or logs, and the business impact. You will receive an acknowledgement within 4 business hours.

STEP 02

Initial Assessment by Support Team

The support team assesses severity and assigns a priority level (P1-P4). For P1/P2 issues, the Delivery Manager is looped in immediately. A formal written response with a resolution plan is provided within 2 business days.

STEP 03

Escalation to Delivery Manager

If the initial response is unsatisfactory or the issue remains unresolved beyond the SLA window, the customer may formally escalate to the named Delivery Manager. The DM takes ownership within 4 business hours of escalation.

STEP 04

Escalation to Business Head

If escalation to the Delivery Manager does not result in a satisfactory resolution within 5 business days, the matter is escalated to the designated Business Head. A formal resolution meeting is convened within 48 hours.

STEP 05

Formal Resolution & Closure

All complaints are resolved with a written closure summary documenting the root cause, actions taken, and any service credits or remedies applied. Closed tickets are retained for 24 months for audit purposes.
